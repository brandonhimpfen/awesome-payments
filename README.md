## Foundations & Standards

- [ISO 20022](https://www.iso20022.org/) – Global standard for financial messaging and payment data.
* [IsoFluent](https://isofluent.com) - Visual ISO 8583 and EMV debugger for protocol inspection and transaction simulation.
- [SWIFT](https://www.swift.com/) – Secure messaging network used for international payments.
- [PCI DSS](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/) – Security standard for handling cardholder data.
- [EMV](https://www.emvco.com/) – Global standard for chip-based payment cards.
- [SEPA](https://www.ecb.europa.eu/paym/integration/retail/sepa/html/index.en.html) – European framework for euro-denominated bank transfers.

## Payment Processors & Gateways

- [Stripe](https://stripe.com/) – Payment processing platform for online and subscription businesses.
- [Adyen](https://www.adyen.com/) – Global payment platform for enterprises and marketplaces.
- [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/) – Online payments and digital wallet platform.
- [Checkout.com](https://www.checkout.com/) – Cloud-native global payment processing platform.
- [Braintree](https://www.braintreepayments.com/) – Payment gateway for web and mobile applications.
- [Worldpay](https://www.worldpay.com/) – Global payment processing and acquiring services.

## Cards & Card Networks

- [Visa](https://www.visa.com/) – Global card network enabling electronic payments.
- [Mastercard](https://www.mastercard.com/) – Worldwide payments and card network.
- [American Express](https://www.americanexpress.com/) – Card network and issuing bank.
- [Discover](https://www.discover.com/) – Payments network and card issuer.
- [UnionPay](https://www.unionpayintl.com/) – International card network based in China.

## Digital Wallets

- [Apple Pay](https://www.apple.com/apple-pay/) – Mobile wallet for contactless and online payments.
- [Google Pay](https://pay.google.com/) – Digital wallet and online payment platform.
- [PayPal Wallet](https://www.paypal.com/) – Digital wallet for online and peer-to-peer payments.
- [Samsung Pay](https://www.samsung.com/pay/) – Mobile payment and digital wallet service.
- [Alipay](https://www.alipay.com/) – Digital wallet and payment platform widely used in Asia.

## Bank Transfers & ACH

- [ACH Network](https://www.nacha.org/) – U.S. electronic funds transfer system.
- [SEPA Credit Transfer](https://www.europeanpaymentscouncil.eu/) – Standardized euro bank transfers.
- [Faster Payments](https://www.fasterpayments.org.uk/) – Near real-time payments in the UK.
- [Interac](https://www.interac.ca/) – Canadian interbank transfer and debit network.
- [Zelle](https://www.zellepay.com/) – Peer-to-peer bank transfer network in the U.S.

## Cross-Border & FX

- [Wise](https://wise.com/) – International money transfers with transparent exchange rates.
- [Payoneer](https://www.payoneer.com/) – Cross-border payments for freelancers and marketplaces.
- [OFX](https://www.ofx.com/) – International money transfer and FX services.
- [Currencycloud](https://www.currencycloud.com/) – APIs for cross-border payments and FX.
- [Airwallex](https://www.airwallex.com/) – Global payments and FX platform for businesses.

## Billing, Subscriptions & Invoicing

- [Stripe Billing](https://stripe.com/billing) – Subscription management and recurring billing platform.
- [Chargebee](https://www.chargebee.com/) – Subscription billing and revenue management.
- [Recurly](https://recurly.com/) – Subscription billing platform for SaaS businesses.
- [Zuora](https://www.zuora.com/) – Enterprise subscription and billing management.
- [FreshBooks](https://www.freshbooks.com/) – Invoicing and payments for small businesses.

## Fraud, Risk & Disputes

- [Stripe Radar](https://stripe.com/radar) – Machine-learning-based fraud prevention for payments.
- [Sift](https://sift.com/) – Fraud detection and risk management platform.
- [Riskified](https://www.riskified.com/) – Chargeback protection and fraud prevention.
- [Feedzai](https://feedzai.com/) – AI-powered fraud detection for financial institutions.
- [Forter](https://www.forter.com/) – Fraud prevention and identity protection for e-commerce.

## Embedded & Marketplace Payments

- [Stripe Connect](https://stripe.com/connect) – Payments infrastructure for marketplaces and platforms.
- [Adyen for Platforms](https://www.adyen.com/platforms) – End-to-end marketplace payment solution.
- [Mangopay](https://www.mangopay.com/) – Payment infrastructure for marketplaces.
- [PayPal Commerce Platform](https://www.paypal.com/business/platform) – Marketplace and platform payments.
- [Square for Platforms](https://squareup.com/platforms) – Embedded payments for platforms and SaaS products.

## Payment APIs & Developer Tools

- [Stripe API](https://stripe.com/docs/api) – APIs for payments, billing, and financial workflows.
- [PayPal Developer](https://developer.paypal.com/) – APIs for PayPal payments and wallets.
- [Adyen API](https://docs.adyen.com/) – APIs for global payment processing.
- [Braintree SDK](https://developer.paypal.com/braintree/docs) – SDKs for integrating card and PayPal payments.
- [Mollie API](https://docs.mollie.com/) – Simple APIs for European payment methods.

## Compliance & Security

- [PCI DSS](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/) – Mandatory security standards for card payments.
- [PSD2](https://www.ecb.europa.eu/paym/integration/retail/psd2/html/index.en.html) – EU regulation governing electronic payments.
- [Strong Customer Authentication (SCA)](https://www.eba.europa.eu/regulation-and-policy/payment-services-and-electronic-money/strong-customer-authentication-and-common-and-secure-open-standards-of-communication) – Authentication requirements under PSD2.
- [Tokenization](https://www.visa.com/run-your-business/small-business-tools/payment-technology/tokenization.html) – Replacing sensitive payment data with tokens.
- [3D Secure](https://www.emvco.com/emv-technologies/3d-secure/) – Additional authentication layer for card-not-present transactions.
